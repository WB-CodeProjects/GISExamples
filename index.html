GIS Webmaps

by William Buffett

Three interactive maps exported from QGIS and hosted on GitHub Pages. Choose a project below to launch the live webmap.

Map 1: <Taupō Moho Depths>

An interactive map of the Taupō region showing seismic station locations, volcanic centres, and representative Moho depths. The data have been generalised for illustrative purposes and do not represent the exact research dataset..

Map 2: <Lidar to DEM Example>
